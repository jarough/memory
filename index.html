Welcome to Memorise, a simple memory matching puzzle game.

The game is simple. You will have 3 seconds to memorise the card symbols shown on screen, after which they will turn over.

There are 5 different symbols: Club, Diamond, Exclamation, Heart, Question and Spade.

Your goal is to match each card with its pair - that is if you can remember! Each correct pair will increase your score by 1 up to a total of 6.

Good Luck!
